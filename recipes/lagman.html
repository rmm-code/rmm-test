Lagman

Description

Lagman is the best meal in the world!

Ingredients

For Noodles:

  • 500 grams (1.1 lbs) of flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • Water (enough to make a dough)

For The Sauce:

  • 500 grams (1.1 lbs) of lamb or beef, cut into small pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Shape the Noodles
  3. Cook the Meat
  4. Cook the Vegetables
  5. Combine and Simmer
  6. Boil the Noodles
  7. Assemble the Lagman
