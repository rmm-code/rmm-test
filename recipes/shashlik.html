Shashlik

Description

Shashlik is the best meal in the world!

Ingredients

  • Meat: 500 grams of lamb or beef
  • Rice: 500 grams of long-grain rice
  • Carrots: 3 large carrots
  • Onions: 2 medium onions
  • Garlic: 1 whole head of garlic
  • Oil: 100 ml of vegetable oil or lamb fat
  • Water: 1 liter

Steps

  1. Rinse the Rice
  2. Prepare the Meat and Vegetables
  3. Heat the Oil
  4. Cook the Meat
  5. Add the Onions
  6. Add the Carrot
  7. Add Spices and Water
  8. Add the Rice
  9. Add the Garlic
  10. Cook the Plov
  11. Steam the Plov
  12. Serve the Plov
