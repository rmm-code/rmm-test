Shashlik

Description

Shashlik is the best meal in the world!

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, finely sliced
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup plain yogurt or kefir
  • Juice of 1 lemons
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cumin

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat
  2. Make the Marinade
  3. Marinate the Meat
  4. Prepare the Skewers
  5. Grill the Shashlik
  6. Serve the Shashlik
