Manti

Description

Manti is the best meal in the world!

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water

For the Filling:

  • 500 grams (1.1 lbs) ground lamb or beef
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped

    • Steps

    1. Prepare the Dough
    2. Prepare the Filling
    3. Roll Out the Dough
    4. Prepare the Steamer
    5. Steam the Manti
    6. Serve the Manti
    7. Add Spices and Water
    8. Add the Rice
    9. Add the Garlic
    10. Cook the Plov
    11. Steam the Plov
    12. Serve the Plov
